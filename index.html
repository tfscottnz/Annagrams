How to play

Choose 4 to 9 letters. Make words of at least three letters using each tile at most once.

You do not have to find every accepted word. Hit the score target or the word-length targets to complete the puzzle. Extra words still score points.

The answer lists are generated from a filtered English dictionary, with target caps so the game stays kind rather than becoming a lexical excavation pit.
